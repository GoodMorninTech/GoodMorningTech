Some FAQs:

How does this project function if it's 100% free & open-source?
We rely on donations on our GitHub & Ko-fi page along with kind sponsors who help this project. To donate, visit this page and to view our sponsors, visit this page.
How do I get in touch with your team?
You can reach out to us on our Discord server or Twitter account. We also have a GitHub repository where you can contribute to the project. You can write to us at support@goodmorningtech.news, we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
How do I unsubscribe/change my newsletter settings?
You can unsubscribe/change your newsletter settings by clicking on the link at the bottom of the newsletter or by visiting this page.
How do I contribute to this project?
You can contribute to this project by visiting our GitHub repository. If you'd like to write for us, please visit this page. If you'd like to sponsor us, please email us at support@goodmorningtech.news.
