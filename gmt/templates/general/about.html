How we got here:

The Tech Morning Newspaper

04th October 2022

Simon made a post on a Discord Server, where he was looking for collaborators on his project called the Tech Morning Newspaper. Levani, Kappa and Harry were interested in the idea and wanted to help.

Backend Started

10th October 2022

We decided that we should use Flask for the backend and then we started to modify the existing codebase for it. Soon after the first email verification & sign up systems were implemented.

Renaming the project

10th October 2022

This project was renamed to Good Morning Tech by Harry. We also came up with our catchy tagline! “Your daily dose of tech, straight to your inbox!”

First Logo Prototypes

21st October 2022

First Landing Page Prototype

28nd October 2022

The first landing page prototype was completed and pushed. Little did we know, we were far from over! We also managed to push some other pages like the 404 page, unsubscribe page and the subscribe page in the span of the next one week.

New Readme.md

5th November 2022

We got our first readme.md in place, and we are still using this one.

Confirm Email

11th November

Levani, our backend developer managed to get the email verification system to work with styling! Although the first iteration of the backend for this verification was pushed in October in a commit made by Kappa.

Rebranding

16th November 2022

On 16th November, Harry rebranded the logo from the old one to the one we are using right now!
